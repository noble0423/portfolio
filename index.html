About me

I am a user-focused Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for integrating technologies in order to solve complex, real-world problems and improve upon existing methods.

While living out in sunny Southern California in early 2018, I embarked onto a new horizon in my professional life and transitioned to a career in Web Development. I graduated from the Full Stack Coding Boot Camp at the UCLA Extension where I excelled in building numerous web applications by integrating multiple languages, platforms, and technologies.

Shortly after graduating, I took those newly acquired skills and moved back to my hometown of Houston, TX, bringing with me a passion for learning and over 12 years of sales experience from a variety of complex, technical industries. Soon after my arrival, an opportunity arose that allowed me to join the Instructional Staff with the Full Stack Coding Boot Camp at the University of Texas (Houston campus).

It was during that time that I started my own freelance Web Development company, RMN Web Design LLC., and began taking on projects that allowed me continue learning, add new capabilities to my knowledge base, build out my portfolio, and tackle the many obstacles involved with very customer-specific Web Dev needs and requirements.

Today, I continue to deliver a world-class customer experience to my clients at RMN Web Design LLC, as well as teach the newest members of the Web Dev community at the Coding Boot Camp at Rice University.
